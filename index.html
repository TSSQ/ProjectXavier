Frequently asked

How does Xavier understand what I type?

Type something like “coffee 4.80” or “lunch 12 yesterday” and Xavier’s on-device assistant reads it into an amount, category, and date for you to confirm. All of this happens on your iPhone — nothing is sent to us.

Where is my financial data stored?

On your device only. Xavier keeps everything in a local database that is encrypted at rest, with no account to sign up for and no analytics. We don’t collect your expenses, and there is no server of ours for them to pass through.

How do backups work?

Backups go to your own iCloud — not to us. You can back up and restore your data so it moves with you to a new device, and it never touches infrastructure we operate.

What is “Bring Your Own Key” (BYOK)?

An optional setting for people who want to use their own OpenAI or Anthropic account to help parse expenses. It’s off by default. When you turn it on, requests go directly from your device to that provider using your own API key — Xavier never sees the key or the text. Leave it off and everything stays fully on-device.

How do I lock the app with Face ID?

In Settings, turn on the Face ID lock. It’s optional and off by default; when on, Xavier asks for Face ID before your finances appear.

I found a bug or something looks wrong

Please email taedevelops@gmail.com with your device model, iOS version, and what you were doing. Screenshots help a lot. We read every message.

Privacy

Xavier collects no personal data. Your expenses stay on your device, encrypted at rest, and backups go only to your own iCloud. There are no accounts and no tracking. Optional Bring-Your-Own-Key requests, if you enable them, go straight from your device to your chosen AI provider under your own key.
