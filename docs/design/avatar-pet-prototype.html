Xavier — animated avatar prototype

A ChatGPT-pet-style blob. It's always alive (gentle breathing + blinking) and reacts to the assistant's state. Tap a state to preview. This is the motion language we'd implement with Reanimated (Phase 1).

✦✦✦
idle — breathing & blinking
How states map to the app:
idle — default rest.
listening — user is typing or scanning a receipt (input focused / camera open).
thinking — busy while the AI proxy parses.
happy — a draft was saved (“Saved! Anything else?”).
confused — parse/save error, or a clarifying question.
