Settings — fold the avatar controls draft

The Assistant avatar block used to render its full style picker and colour swatches inline, taking a big chunk of the Settings list. Now it’s folded behind a tappable header showing the current selection (preview swatch + “Blob · Mint”) and a chevron. Tapping unfolds the controls; tapping again folds them back.
